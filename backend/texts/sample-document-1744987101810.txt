Document Title: Google Company Overview

Google was founded by Larry Page and Sergey Brin while they were Ph.D. students at Stanford
University. It was incorporated as a privately held company on September 4, 1998.

The company's mission is to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and
useful.

Headquarters: Mountain View, Califomia, United States.

As of 2022, Google is a subsidiary of Alphabet Inc., which reported a revenue of $282.8 billion in the
same year.

Core Products:

- Google Search

- Android

- YouTube

- Google Cloud
